Endangered Species
California Condor
Bird
Habitat: Coastal mountains and forests of California and Baja California.
Threats: Lead poisoning from ingesting bullet fragments in carrion, microtrash ingestion, habitat destruction.
Conservation Status: Critically Endangered.
Conservation Efforts: Captive breeding programs, lead ammunition bans, and habitat protection.
Endangered Species
Kakapo
Bird
Habitat: Forests of New Zealand.
Threats: Predation by introduced species (cats, rats), habitat destruction.
Conservation Status: Critically Endangered
Conservation Efforts: Intensive management, including predator control, captive breeding, and translocation to predator-free islands.
Endangered Species
Philippine Eagle
Bird
Habitat: Tropical rainforests of the Philippines.
Threats: Deforestation, hunting, and habitat fragmentation.
Conservation Status: Critically Endangered.
Conservation Efforts: Habitat protection, community education programs, and captive breeding.
Endangered Species
Spix’s Macaw
Bird
Habitat: Riparian forests along the Rio São Francisco in Brazil.
Threats: Habitat destruction, illegal pet trade.
Conservation Status: Critically Endangered (possibly extinct in the wild).
Conservation Efforts: Captive breeding and reintroduction programs, habitat restoration.
